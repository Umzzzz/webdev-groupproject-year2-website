Employability Hub

The careers & employability service is located in the ground floor of the new Business School. If you need some career advice, CV revision or you just want to improve your interview skills drop in between 9:00am and 5:00pm on weekdays and book and appointment. They are running several workshops, which are improving the employability of students:

  • How to write winning CVs
  • Job hunting using social media
  • LinkedIn from amateur to pro
  • How to survive assessment centres and get the job
  • Do you want to know what is coming up? Contact the employability HUB

Student Support Officer

David Peters is the Student Support Officer at the faculty. He is able to give you a hand in any University related issues and is running the following workshops:

  • Academic writing skills
  • Referencing and avoiding plagiarism
  • Applying critical thinking
  • Managing your time
  • Presentation skills

Learn a foreign language

Uniwide is an exciting opportunity to learn and develop your language skills. Students can study a uniwide language as part of their degree as a 15 credit or 30 credit unit. The programmes are available on different levels and examples of the languages available include:

  • German
  • Italian
  • Spanish
  • Japanese
  • + many more
