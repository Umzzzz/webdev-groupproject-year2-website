Our Department

  • Back to Courses

At the Journalism, Information and Communications Department, we are a unique department that is situated on the second floor of the Geoffrey Manton building. One of the most established department On the All Saints Campus we have over 8,000 students on the campus and our department is a part of that community. The department’s main area for seminars is the MRL on the second floor. You cannot miss it! Here in this department we are dedicated, in nurturing the potential of our students doing a degree on any of the routes in this department. The department offers courses for both undergraduates and postgraduates; we have part time and full time options for either course type you prefer. Some of the popular courses are below:

Under Graduate:

  • Information and Communications – Full/Part time

  • Information and Communications degrees with a Foundation Year - Full Time

  • BA (Hons) Multimedia Journalism - Full Time

  • BSc (Hons) Web Development - Full/Part time

Post Graduate:

  • MSc Information Management - Full/Part time

  • MA Library and Information Management - Full/Part time

  • MA Multimedia Journalism - Full/Part time
