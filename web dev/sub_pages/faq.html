FAQ

  • Back to Help

When do the courses start?

The courses available have an enrolment date in September. You will get more information about the day you start in an acceptance letter.

How long is a course?

Each course is 3 years full-time and 6 years part-time.

Can I get specific information about a course?

If you can’t find enough information on the courses page, you can always contact the department directly.

Is there a student union?

The union provides a variety of social, cultural and sporting activities. There is a new student union building very close to campus offering more entertainment, a place where you can socialize, relax and play ping pong!

Will University help me in my career?

Yes! A large percentage of graduates from the department go straight into employment. The skills and knowledge gained while studying will benefit any career path you may choose.

I’m in, what next?

You will receive communications from the University with specific information about the process but it is very simple. Using your confirmation letter there are a few steps to follow:

  • 1. Upload your photo

  • 2. Enrol

  • 4. Get your MMU Student Card

  • 5. Attend your first induction session
